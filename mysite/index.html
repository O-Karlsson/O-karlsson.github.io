Resume

Name: Omar Karlsson

Position: Visiting Research Fellow at Duke Univeristy Population Research Institute and Duke Global Health Institute

Experience: Ten years of quantitative research experience using econometrics, demographic methods, and GIS on pooled surveys and demographic data. Over 30 peer-reviewed articles in medical, public health, and demography journals since 2019.

Skills: Expert user of Stata. Experienced user of Python, JavaScript, and Excel. Excellent written and verbal communication skills. I emphasize readability and accessibility, with the goal of having my research be assessable and applicable across disciplines and by decision-makers. I have participated in many successful interdisciplinary collaborations, locally and abroad.

Research interests:I study life expectancy, child health, living standards, health behaviors, and social determinants. The life-course perspective and human development framework motivate my research: wellbeing is advanced by enhancing the capabilities of individuals by promoting opportunities and limiting adversity.

Contact: karlssono@outlook.com
